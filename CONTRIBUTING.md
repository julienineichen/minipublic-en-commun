### What you can contribute

- **Experience feedback**: have you implemented a MiniPublic or a similar method? Share what worked, what was difficult, what you adapted.
- **Local adaptations**: have you adapted the method to a specific context (different competition standards, different country, different scale)? Document it and propose it.
- **Corrections and improvements**: an imprecise formulation, a poorly described step, a missing reference? Flag it or propose a correction.
- **Translations**: would you like to translate the documentation into another language? Get in touch.
- **Facilitation tools**: templates, evaluation grids, facilitation guides ‚Äî any tool tested in practice is valuable.

### How to contribute

1. **Open an issue** to report a problem, ask a question, or propose an improvement before starting work.
2. **Fork the repository** and work on a dedicated branch.
3. **Submit a pull request** clearly describing what you changed and why.
4. Contributions are reviewed by the En Commun team before integration.
